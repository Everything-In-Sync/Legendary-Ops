THE GAME

• We love gaming

The team at Legendary Ops loves gaming just as much as you do. That's why we are so passionate about bringing the best immersive gaming experiences to life with our state of the art laser tag equipment.

Team deathmatch, sabotage, capture the flag, infected and battle royale are just a few of the game types we offer. Hardcore options for advanced players can include things like friendly fire to bring the realism to a new level.
