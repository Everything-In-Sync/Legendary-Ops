THE GAME

• We love gaming

Assemble your team or come play as an individual to put your leadership, competitiveness, and teamwork skills to the test in our interactive game modes like team deathmatch, sabotage, capture the flag, infected, battle royal and more.

With unlimited ways to customize our game modes, we can up the stakes and realism with hardcore play mechanics like friendly fire to customize your experience. No two games will feel alike creating a huge replay ability that is matched by none. Level up through game play and earn medals, perks, kill streaks rewards.  Learn more below.

Level Up and Earn Rewards


We understand how important it is to have a real feel of achievement and progression.

  With our top of its class laser tag software you can now earn perks like unique weapon types, upgrades, faster reload times, increased armor, game discounts, and even exclusive Legendary Ops Merchandise.  The CallSign app allows you to have real time tracking of your leveling progress, and stats like kill death ratio, accuracy, and even see how many times you shot your arch nemesis playing across from you. Along the way you can customize your load out and unlock new guns that may feature faster reload times or heavier damage. Even earn more experience points to level up through sharing your score and achievements on facebook. Work your way to the very rare Legendary status which will allow you to become a part of the Legendary Ops team with exclusive perks like creating and naming your own gun loadout, invited to exclusive events, test and create new game modes with the team, and more.  

Unlock Medals and Achievements


Nothing feels more rewarding than to be on a great kill streak and to be awarded a kill streak reward like a UAV to see all the enemies on the battlefield or a Nuke to obliterate them and score high points for your team.

  Play hard and be awarded medals through in game accomplishments like most kills, least amount of deaths, double kills, and many more. These achievements can be tracked and shown off through the CallSign app.   Download the CallSign app here and create an account to track your progress.
