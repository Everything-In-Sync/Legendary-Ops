Who and What?

Legendary Ops is a mobile laser tag gaming company that does private, public, and corporate laser tag events. Our team is focused on bringing an immersive one of a kind experience that challenges their customers with new and innovative laser tag technology.  We are gamers and enjoy gaming just as much as the next person so we are passionate and excited about bringing all the great things gamers experience through games like “Call of Duty” and “Fortnite” to life with our game. No more brainless shot and kill, with our gaming you will have to tactically devise a plan and skillfully deploy your plan to come out on top.  Good Luck, and see you on the battlefield!
