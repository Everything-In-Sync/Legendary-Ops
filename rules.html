RULES OF PLAY

Full

Each player must read, accept and agree to abide by these rules prior to play.
By reading them you are accepting them and agreeing to abide by them.

There are three areas at each engagement regardless of venue or type:

Headquarters (HQ)
Registration, payment (if public event), game and sound system administration
Armory
Game equipment location, briefing, staging
Arena
Field of play

Each engagement where new players are present will begin with a safety briefing, rule review and equipment demonstration at the Armory Players will return to the Armory at the end of each engagement Players must be 8 years or older and taller than 48 inches Legendary Ops must be notified prior to playing if any guests, playing or not, suffer from the following: Asthma, epilepsy/seizures caused by fog or strobe lights and/or any other health condition All players must wear a skull cap (a new skull cap is provided by Legendary Ops), balaclava/ski mask, or head wrap that creates barrier between the head and headband sensor Shoes are required â€“ no sandals, bare feet, flip-flops or open-toed shoes No foul or abusive language No running No climbing on or under obstacles provided by Legendary Ops or any other object in the environment whether provided by Legendary Ops or not (Example: Trees, buildings, vehicles, etc.) No barrel roles, superman-type dives, or jumping over obstacles No slamming, bumping, or moving obstacles No physical contact between players Laser must be held with both hands. Each laser is equipped with a sling, we strongly encourage its use (IF YOU BREAK, YOU BUY IT) Headband sensors must be properly worn around the crown of the head and must be properly adjusted to maintain location during play Players cannot cover headband sensors with their hands or clothing Players are not allowed to leave the designated playing area with equipment Players should not aim at or engage bystanders outside of the playing area No intentional abuse of the equipment or obstacles All unsafe behavior will be identified and corrected by the game specialist. Repeat offenders will be removed from games. Physical or verbal threats against another player will result in an ejection from the game. Customers hosting an event are responsible for selecting a safe location and correspond with Legendary Ops. Game specialists are responsible for identifying and marking off or removing any safety hazards as well as informing the customer. The designated playing area will be clearly defined to ensure that all players are safe and easily identified as players in a game. Game specialists will not be penalized for enforcing the safety rules, safety is our number one priority. The game specialist has the right to terminate any engagement due to unsafe conditions for unexpected or unavoidable hazards, including but not limited to, weather, the presence of illegal drugs, excessive alcohol use, injury to a guest, abusive player activity, rule violations, etc. No refunds will be provided for players or customers ejected from the game
