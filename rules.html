RULES OF PLAY

- Quick Reference -

In order to ensure that Legendary Ops is the safest and most fun laser tag experience you can have
we have a few ground rules that all participants must follow.

Our basic laser tag rules and regulations are:

No Running
No Climbing
No Physical Contact Between Players
Hold Laser with Both Hands

Players must be careful maneuvering around arena walls, obstacles and each other
Legendary Ops will not tolerate any running, climbing or personal contact in the arena. There is a zero-tolerance
policy for this rule. If at any time the players of any age or any group do not follow the rules and regulations of
Legendary Ops, they will be ejected immediately. If any one player is running, climbing or making overt physical
contact, the game will be ended immediately without warning.
These are the Rules of Play quick reference. By reading them you are accepting them and agreeing to abide by them.
The full set of rules, terms and conditions is available at the event HQ, online at www.legendaryops.com and must be
agreed to prior to playing.

RULES OF PLAY

- Full -

Each player must read, accept and agree to abide by these rules prior to play.
By reading them you are accepting them and agreeing to abide by them.

There are three areas at each engagement regardless of venue or type:

Headquarters (HQ)
Registration, payment (if public event), game and sound system administration
Armory
Game equipment location, briefing, staging
Arena
Field of play

Each engagement where new players are present will begin with a safety briefing, rule review and equipment demonstration at the Armory
Players will return to the Armory at the end of each engagement
Players must be 8 years or older and taller than 48 inches
Legendary Ops must be notified prior to playing if any guests, playing or not, suffer from the
following: Asthma, epilepsy/seizures caused by fog or strobe lights and/or any other health condition
All players must wear a skull cap (a new skull cap is provided by Legendary Ops), balaclava/ski
mask, or head wrap that creates barrier between the head and headband sensor
Shoes are required â€“ no sandals, bare feet, flip-flops or open-toed shoes
No foul or abusive language
No running
No climbing on or under obstacles provided by Legendary Ops or any other object in the environment
whether provided by Legendary Ops or not (Example: Trees, buildings, vehicles, etc.)
No barrel roles, superman-type dives, or jumping over obstacles
No slamming, bumping, or moving obstacles
No physical contact between players
Laser must be held with both hands. Each laser is equipped with a sling, we strongly encourage its use
(IF YOU BREAK, YOU BUY IT)
Headband sensors must be properly worn around the crown of the head and must be properly adjusted to
maintain location during play
Players cannot cover headband sensors with their hands or clothing
Players are not allowed to leave the designated playing area with equipment
Players should not aim at or engage bystanders outside of the playing area
No intentional abuse of the equipment or obstacles
All unsafe behavior will be identified and corrected by the game specialist. Repeat offenders will
be removed from games. Physical or verbal threats against another player will result in an ejection
from the game. Customers hosting an event are responsible for selecting a safe location and
correspond with Legendary Ops. Game specialists are responsible for identifying and marking off or
removing any safety hazards as well as informing the customer. The designated playing area will be
clearly defined to ensure that all players are safe and easily identified as players in a game. Game
specialists will not be penalized for enforcing the safety rules, safety is our number one priority.
The game specialist has the right to terminate any engagement due to unsafe conditions for
unexpected or unavoidable hazards, including but not limited to, weather, the presence of illegal
drugs, excessive alcohol use, injury to a guest, abusive player activity, rule violations, etc.
No refunds will be provided for players or customers ejected from the game
